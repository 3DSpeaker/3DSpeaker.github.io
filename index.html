3D-Speaker
Datasets

A large scale multi-Device, multi-Distance, and multi-Dialect audio dataset of human speech

10000+ Speakers

3D-Speaker Datasets contains 10000+ speakers spanning a wide range of different accents, professions and ages.

Various Devices

The device information of all 500k+ utterances have been recorded, like Phone, RecordingPen, PC, Array, etc.

Near & Far field

Provide information of distance between sound source and microphone range from 0.1m to 4m.

Dialect Cover

As a Chinese-based dataset, 3D-Speaker-Datasets covers data of 14 different Mandarin dialects.

Dataset Features

Statistics of Multi-Device

We provide the device information for each utterance including PC, Recording Pen, Array, Phone and IPad.

Statistics of Multi-Dialect

The datasets contains 14 kinds of dialect and covers nearly all dialects in China.

Many far-field utterances

We provide both near-field and far-field data for building robust speaker-verfication model.
